Join the Flame

We're building a team as passionate about great food and hospitality as we are. If you thrive in fast-paced environments and love making people smile, we want to hear from you.

Head Pitmaster

Full-time Senior Urgent

Lead our grill station, manage smoke programs, train junior cooks, and maintain our award-winning BBQ standards. Minimum 5 years experience in smokehouse or BBQ kitchen required.

₦450k – ₦600k /mo

Restaurant Manager

Full-time Management

Oversee daily operations, manage a team of 25+ staff, ensure exceptional guest experiences, and drive revenue growth. Strong leadership and P&L management experience essential.

₦350k – ₦500k /mo

Sous Chef

Full-time Mid-level

Support the Head Chef in menu development, kitchen operations, and quality control. Creative flair and ability to work under pressure in a high-volume environment.

₦200k – ₦280k /mo

Front of House Supervisor

Full-time Supervisory

Lead our service team during shifts, handle guest relations, coordinate with kitchen, and ensure smooth service flow. Excellent communication skills required.

₦150k – ₦200k /mo

Mixologist / Bartender

Full-time Creative

Craft signature cocktails, manage bar inventory, and create memorable drink experiences that pair perfectly with our BBQ menu. Cocktail competition experience is a plus.

₦120k – ₦180k /mo

Marketing & Social Media Intern

Internship Entry-level

Assist with content creation, social media management, event photography, and brand campaigns. Perfect for creative students or recent graduates passionate about food media.

₦50k – ₦70k /mo
